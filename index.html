How to use this calculator?

Just like any other calculator! Click numbers and operators or use your keyboard.

Keyboard shortcuts:

  • Numbers 0-9 and decimal point (.)
  • Operators: + - * / and Enter (=)
  • Backspace to delete
  • Escape to clear (press twice to clear everything)
