How to use this calculator?

Just like any other calculator!
Click numbers, operators and functions buttons or use your keyboard

Keyboard shortcuts:

  • Numbers 0-9 and decimal point (.)
  • Operators: + - * / and Enter (=)
  • Backspace to delete
  • Escape to clear (press twice to clear everything)

There's also a Dev View if you're curious to peek inner values
